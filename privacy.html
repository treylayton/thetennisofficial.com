Privacy Policy

Effective March 26, 2026

The Tennis Official collects no personal data. Everything runs on your device.

Overview

The Tennis Official ("the App") is published by Centrifical, Inc. We built this app with a simple privacy principle: your data is yours. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No cookies or similar technologies
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

On-Device Processing

All features of The Tennis Official—including decision trees and AI-powered chat—run entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. Your questions, rulings, and interactions never leave your phone. There are no servers, no cloud processing, and no external API calls.

Subscriptions

The App offers a subscription managed entirely through Apple's App Store. All billing, payment processing, and subscription management are handled by Apple according to their privacy practices. Centrifical, Inc. does not receive or store any payment information.

For information about how Apple handles your purchase data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The Tennis Official does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. There is nothing to opt out of because there is nothing collecting your data.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data from any user, we inherently comply with COPPA and similar regulations. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date. Given that we collect no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact
